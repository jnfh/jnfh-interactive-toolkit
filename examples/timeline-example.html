How to Use the Universal Timeline

  • The timeline at the bottom controls both players simultaneously
  • Click the play button to start all audio elements in sync
  • Drag the timeline slider to seek to any position
  • All registered players will stay synchronized during playback
  • Use timeline.registerPlayer(id, player) to add more players
